Who We Are

Theo's Workshop is a car maintenance and repair workshop based in Roodepoort, Gauteng. We provide professional mechanical expertise at our workshop with efficient turnaround and transparent service.

Founded on a passion for vehicles and a belief that car repairs should be stress-free and transparent, Theo's Workshop has built a reputation for honest pricing, quality workmanship, and reliable service across Gauteng.

Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex engine repair, our goal is simple: get your vehicle running perfectly, wherever you are.
